About
Miss Hacks is a hackathon for all high school girls interested in making their project ideas come to life. This all girls high school hackathon lasts for 12 hours. Knowing how to code is not required in Miss Hacks, as we provide various workshops to teach those interested.

Our Goal
We strive to encourage more future girl entrepreneurs, leaders, and computer scientists. As there is a great number of men dominating the field of computer science, we want them to feel welcomed in the tech world.
FAQs
Who Can participate?
Any girl that is currently enrolled in a highschool may participate.
How Much Does it Cost?
It's Free! Free Food too!
What if I don't Know how to Code?
That's definetly ok. We wil provide workshops for girls who have never coded before to help you get started on your creation.
What if I don't have a Team?
That's Ok! Most people don't have their teams decided until the day of the hackathon where they meet new people! The maximum number of people in a team is 4.
What Should I Bring?
Bring a laptop of any kind and a charger since you will be there for 12 Hours!
When and Where is it?
Miss Hack's first hackathon is on April 14th and the location is yet to be announced but it will most definetly be somewhere in Massachusetts.
